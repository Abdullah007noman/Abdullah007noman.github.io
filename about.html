About Me

I’m a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at The University of Texas at Arlington,specializing in Wireless Communication. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Engineering from National Institute of Technology, India. I am a member of the TWiST (Transformative Wireless Systems and Technology) Lab and supervised by Dr. Debashri Roy. My research explores cutting-edge areas including Space-Air-Ground Integrated Networks (SAGIN),Open RAN (Radio Access Networks), and signal processing, with the goal of developing intelligent, reliable, and sustainable communication systems for the modern world.Driven by curiosity and a passion for innovation, I aim to contribute to technologies that enhance global connectivity and redefine how we communicate across terrestrial and space networks.
